Ippindo Ramen House

Ramen (ラーメン) is a staple in Japanese culture. Moreso a cultural icon. Every region in Japan prides itself on its own variation of Ramen. From Fukuoka(福岡市), infamously known for Tonkotsu(豚骨)long boiled pork bone broth to Hokkaido(北海道), widely known for its Miso(みそ) Ramen. Ippindo Ramen House welcomes Ramen lovers a seat to travel coast to coast through high quality ingredients, traditional Ramen flavors and appreciation for the Ramen Cuisine.
